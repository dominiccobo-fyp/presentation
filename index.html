What did we do?

  • We draw inspiration from the principles of LSP
Re-use the principles of the Language Server Protocol - LSP
  • All logic is contained with the daemon.
  • It understands how to send queries for the provider.
  • It understands how to handle the results from the queries.
  • The IDE simply acts as a presentation layer.
  • What are providers?
  • They have mined an opinionated view of the data and, or information available for a topic.
  • Many providers for one topic.
